Our Story

Novel Idea is Kingston's independent bookstore. We sell new fiction & non-fiction books, including hard-to-find titles by local authors.

Owned by Oscar & Joanna Malan, we have been serving the Kingston community since 1988. We also offer calendars, agendas, day planners, greeting cards, and journals.

Not in stock? We're always happy to place special orders for our customers.
